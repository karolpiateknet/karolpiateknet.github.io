# Privacy Policy for Focus Rewards

**Effective Date: November 16, 2025**

This Privacy Policy describes how an independent developer ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and shares information in connection with your use of the Focus Rewards mobile application (the "App").

## 1. Developer Information & Contact

**Data Controller (Application Provider):** An independent developer

**Contact Email for Privacy Questions:** support@swiftyfriday.com

## 2. Information We Collect and Its Purpose

The App is designed to minimize data collection. We only collect data necessary for app functionality, diagnostics, and compliance.

### 2.1. Categories of Personal Information Collected

| Data Type | Collected? (Yes/No) | Examples & CCPA Categories | Purpose |
|-----------|---------------------|----------------------------|---------|
| Identifiers | Yes | IP Address (via Firebase), Device ID | For diagnostics, crash reporting, and distinguishing users for A/B testing. |
| Financial/Purchase Data | Yes | Subscription Status, Purchase History | To validate and manage your Apple In-App Subscriptions. |
| Usage Data | Yes | App launches, screens visited, feature interaction | To diagnose crashes, analyze performance, and improve the user experience. |
| Coarse Location Data | Yes | Country/City derived from IP Address | Used for general trend analysis (e.g., "Where is the app most popular?") and legal compliance. |
| User Content | No | User-generated content (e.g., custom modes, rewards) is stored locally only on your device. | Not collected or transmitted by us. |

### 2.2. Location Data Clarification

We do not collect precise geographical location (GPS). However, Firebase Analytics and Crashlytics automatically collect and process the user's IP address, which is used to infer a Coarse Location (city and country) for diagnostic and analytics purposes.

## 3. Third-Party Services, Tracking, and Consent

We use third-party services for essential app management.

### 3.1. Third-Party Services

We utilize the following Google Firebase services. These services operate under their own privacy policies:

- **Firebase Analytics (Usage Data, Coarse Location):** Collects anonymized data on app usage, product interaction, and performance. [Privacy Policy](https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy)
- **Firebase Crashlytics (Diagnostics, Identifiers):** Collects non-personal crash logs, stack traces, and device state information to diagnose and fix technical bugs. [Privacy Policy](https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy)
- **Firebase Remote Config/A/B Testing (Usage Data):** Used to change the behavior and appearance of the app and to run A/B tests and feature flags. This uses non-personal device identifiers to segment users into different testing groups. [Privacy Policy](https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy)

For more information about how Google handles data collected through Firebase services, please review [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

### 3.2. Apple's App Tracking Transparency (ATT)

If, in the future, we decide to use data for purposes defined as "Tracking" under Apple's guidelines (e.g., linking your data with third-party data for targeted advertising), we will first present you with the native Apple tracking consent popup (ATT) and will only proceed if you provide explicit consent.

## 4. Financial and Transaction Data

When you purchase an Apple In-App Subscription, the transaction is processed entirely by Apple's App Store payment system. We do not receive or store your credit card details, billing address, or other sensitive payment information.

We receive notification from Apple regarding the status of your subscription (e.g., purchase date, expiration date, and which product was purchased) solely to verify and manage your access to paid features within the App.

## 5. GDPR Compliance for EU/EEA/UK Users

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), the United Kingdom, or Switzerland, the following terms apply:

### 5.1. Legal Basis for Processing

We process your Personal Data on the basis of Legitimate Interests for the purposes of:

- Diagnosing and fixing crashes (improving the product).
- Analyzing usage to improve the user experience and feature set.
- Processing financial data necessary for the performance of the subscription contract.

### 5.2. International Data Transfers

The data collected is stored on servers provided by Apple and Google (Firebase), which may be located outside of the EU/EEA. When we transfer your data internationally, we ensure it is protected by implementing appropriate safeguards, such as reliance on the European Commission's Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) or other legally required mechanisms.

### 5.3. Your Data Protection Rights (GDPR)

You have the right to:

- Access your Personal Data.
- Rectification of inaccurate data.
- Erasure of your data (the "right to be forgotten").
- Restriction of processing in certain circumstances.
- Data portability (receiving your data in a structured, commonly used format).
- Object to processing based on legitimate interests.
- Lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the email provided in Section 1.

## 6. CCPA/CPRA Compliance for California Residents

If you are a California resident, the following terms apply:

### 6.1. No Sale or Sharing of Personal Information

We do not sell Personal Information (PI) for monetary or other valuable consideration, nor do we share PI for cross-context behavioral advertising, as defined under the CCPA/CPRA.

### 6.2. Consumer Rights (CCPA/CPRA)

You have the right to request:

- **Right to Know:** Disclosure of the specific pieces and categories of PI we have collected about you.
- **Right to Delete:** Deletion of PI we have collected, subject to certain exceptions.
- **Right to Correct:** Correction of inaccurate PI we maintain about you.
- **Right to Opt-Out:** The right to opt-out of the sale or sharing of your Personal Information (not applicable as we do not sell or share PI).

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the email provided in Section 1.

## 7. Data Security, Retention, and Changes

**Security:** We use commercially reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect the information collected from misuse and loss.

**Retention:** We retain data only as long as necessary for the purposes outlined in this policy, to manage your subscription, or as required by law.

**Policy Updates:** We may update this Privacy Policy. We will notify users of any material changes by posting the new policy in the App Store and updating the "Effective Date" at the top of this document.
